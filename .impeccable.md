## Design Context

### Users
Romanian car buyers and sellers. Buyers want to know if a listed price is fair before negotiating. Sellers want to price competitively. Secondary: dealers and analysts monitoring market trends. Used in a browser, typically desktop, during research sessions before a car purchase decision.

### Brand Personality
Confident, data-forward, no-nonsense. Three words: **precise · authoritative · honest**.
Tagline: "Buy smart. Sell smart." — speaks to both buyers and sellers in four words.

### Aesthetic Direction
Uber-inspired confident minimalism. Black/white dominant with Inter as the type system. No decorative color in chrome — charts are the only source of color. Pill-shaped interactive elements (999px radius). Cards with whisper shadow, no colored borders. Think: Bloomberg terminal for normal people, or a financial product that happens to be about cars.

Light theme (users browse during day, comparing car listings).

Anti-references: no colored admin dashboard feel, no blue-button SaaS, no glassmorphism, no AI-product purple gradients.

### Design Principles
1. **Data is the hero** — real numbers should be prominent, not decorative stats
2. **No color tax** — color earns its place; charts are the only exception
3. **Pill everything interactive** — consistent 999px radius on all buttons and chips
4. **Confident whitespace** — generous spacing signals quality and trustworthiness
5. **Type does the heavy lifting** — hierarchy through size and weight, not color or decoration
